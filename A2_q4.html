Welcome to Mahan's Adoption Center, A staple in the Montreal area, we pride ourselves in taking care of our clients as well as our animals.

Mission

Our mission is to match stray animals with their perfect families and guide new pet owners to comfortably integrate their new friend into their household Please refer to the navigation menu on the left to get started!

History

After the pandemic, many people who adopted pets out of boredom decided to abandon their pets, leaving many pets without a home. on July 14th 2022, Mahanaim Yo decided to build his own foundation to save the animals. Today, Mahan's adoption center is the number 2 animal shelter in the NDG area.
